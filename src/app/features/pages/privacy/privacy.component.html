1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies only to the {{ companyInfo.name }} website at noble-wave.com. It explains how this marketing website handles information about its visitors.

It does not apply to applications we build for clients. When {{ companyInfo.name }} develops a web or mobile application for a customer, that application has its own separate privacy policy provided by the customer (the "data controller" of that app's user data).

2. What We Do Not Collect

This website is a static marketing site. To be transparent about what we deliberately avoid:

  • We do not run any analytics, tracking, or advertising scripts.
  • We do not set any tracking cookies.
  • We do not have user accounts, login, or registration.
  • We do not host any contact form that submits to a server.
  • We do not process payments on this site.

3. Information You Send Us Directly

The only way to contact us through this site is by clicking an email or phone link, which opens your own email client or phone app. If you choose to email or call us, we will receive whatever information you include — typically your name, email address or phone number, and the content of your message.

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to a marketing list and we do not share it with third parties.

4. Local Storage

This website uses your browser's localStorage to remember a single preference: your chosen theme (light or dark mode). This value never leaves your browser, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and contains no personal data. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Resources

To render properly, this site loads a few resources from third-party content delivery networks. When your browser fetches these resources, the third party may automatically receive your IP address, user agent, and the URL of the page that loaded them:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) — for typography.
  • jsDelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — for technology icons.

These services have their own privacy policies, which we do not control. We do not pass any additional information to them ourselves.

6. Hosting and Server Logs

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. As with any web host, GitHub may automatically log standard request information (such as IP address and user agent) for security and operational purposes. We do not operate the GitHub Pages infrastructure and do not have direct access to those logs. See GitHub's own privacy policy for details.

7. External Links

Our footer and contact sections contain links to social media profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub, Facebook) and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their policies before sharing any information with them.

8. How Long We Keep Information

For email correspondence you initiate with us, we retain messages in our inbox for as long as necessary to handle your inquiry and for our reasonable business records. You may request deletion of past correspondence at any time by writing to us.

9. Your Rights

Because this site collects no personal information automatically, most data-protection rights apply only to email or phone correspondence you have voluntarily sent us. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of that information.
  • Object to or restrict our use of it.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

10. Children

This website is intended for a business audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a minor has sent us information, please contact us so we can delete it.
